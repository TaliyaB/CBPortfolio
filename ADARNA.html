ADARNA

An entry to the 16th Smart Wireles Engineering Education Program(SWEEP) Innovations and Excellence Awards
Contribution: Data management, Machine Learning

The project was inspired after the typhoon Tisoy wrecked havoc in the Bicol Region. We were stranded in our apartment with no means of communication because all of the service providers were inoperative. It was only after three days since the onset that we heard helicopters surveying the area. Hence, the idea of ADARNA was concieved to aid in rapid damage assessment and information dissemination.
