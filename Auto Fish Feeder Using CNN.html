Auto Fish Feeder Using Convolutional Neural Network

Conducted in Bicol University Polangui Campus from August 2019-May 2020
Project Duration: 10 months
Contribution: Data management , Machine Learning

Tilapia farming is one of the main sources of income in our locality. One contributor to the mortality rate of the fishes is the excessive feeding, which gravely affects the quality produced. The mechanism of our device was based on published jounals which indicate that Nile Tilapia has the tendency to swim up the water surface if it is hungry. The project is built upon this premise. This is a continuation of our adviser's research entitled Feeding Behavior Classification of Nile Tilapia (Oreochromis niloticus) using Convolutional Neural Network.
